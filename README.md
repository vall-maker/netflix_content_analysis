NETFLIX CONTENT ANALYSIS

OBJECTIVES

Analyse Netflix content to identify trends in content types and genres, and provide recomendations.

KEY QUESTIONS

- Are there more movies or tv shows in Netflix?
- Are Netflix focussing more on movies or tv shows?
- Are Netflix's newer content more common?
- Have their content increased over time?
- Which countries produce the most contents on netflix?
- Is Netflix dominated by certain areas?
- What genre appears most?
- Are certain genre growing?

KEY FINDINGS

- Movies dominate the platform
- Movies are still the major focus
- Content has grown significantly over time
- The USA produces the most content
- Comedy is the most common genre
- Action and animation are rapidly growing"
 
INSIGHT

Netflix's growth strategy appears to rely on rapidely expanding it's content library, primarily through movies, while increasingly investing in tv shows to improve long-term user engagement. although the platform is still dominated by content fron the USA, the continual rise of asian content production signals a shift towards global market expansion. at the same time, the growth of action and animation genres suggests and evolving audience preference towards more dynamic and engaging content.
